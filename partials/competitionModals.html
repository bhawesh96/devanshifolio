Programme : To design a writer’s retreat on a bridge that spans a 50 m wide river

Design : The bridge is an infinite loop of experience consisting of 12 rooms for writers and a discussion area. The outer ring serves as the work place with variety of workpods and interesting views around keeping them connected to the water flowing beneath and mountains around.

Concept : : My bridge is a story and all the built mass are the characters. Like every story, this one has some different idea to propose. A mix of themes and combination of the unexpected. It doesnt have a protagonist or an ending. Its an experience in itself. Its a journey.
When a writer resides here, temporarily, comes here with a target. Living amongst the story and characters , one ends up being a part of that experience.

Tiny House Design Competition 2017 BY Volume zero

Programme : To design a 250 sq ft mobile house for anybody which includes living area,sleeping area cooking and dining ,toilet and a workspace.

Design : The 250 sq-ft tiny mobile house is built inside a shipping container which is cut 8 m long along the length. A shipping container is one of the most sustainable materials and is treated with foam boards for insulation. It has a foldable bed for the artist, toilet, kitchen and his workspace facing towards the french windows which admits the transient outsides within and stays connected to the exterior surroundings all the time.

Concept : An Artist who is inspired by the settings he perceives, who travels the world to attain creative impulses and creates memories on canvases - is the user of this tiny house. . A minimalistic design proposal which plays around one of the Gestalt’s logic that white interiors looks spacious & helps the artist to feel the house more comfortable like his canvas. The artist moves around different places with his abode at the back , painting down his memories with his brushes and earns his livelihood by exhibiting his artwork at different places.
